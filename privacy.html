KeepTabs — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

KeepTabs does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to us or to any third party. Period.

What data the extension handles

When you choose to save tabs, KeepTabs stores the page titles and URLs of those tabs locally on your own device, using Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your computer. We have no servers and no way to see it.

No accounts, no tracking

There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Permissions we use, and why

  • tabs — to read the title and URL of the tabs in your current window, only when you press "save".
  • storage — to save your folders locally on your device.

Backup

The backup button exports a JSON file directly to your own computer. We never receive or see this file.

Data deletion

Your data lives only on your device. Deleting a folder, or removing the extension, removes the data. We hold nothing to delete on our side.
